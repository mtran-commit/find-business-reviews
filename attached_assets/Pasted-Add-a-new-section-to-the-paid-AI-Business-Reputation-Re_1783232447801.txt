Add a new section to the paid AI Business Reputation Report called:

Platform Checklist

Purpose:
Show business owners which platforms they should check, monitor and improve based on their business category.

Place this section after:
Platform-by-Platform Comparison

and before:
AI Customer Sentiment Analysis

The section should include:
- platform name
- relevance for this business
- current status
- recommended action
- priority level

Use a table layout.

Table columns:
Platform
Relevant for this business?
Current Status
Recommended Action
Priority

Google

Current Status: Active, strong rating
Recommended Action: Keep collecting recent reviews and respond to all reviews
Priority: High

Facebook
Relevant: Yes — useful for local reputation and community proof
Current Status: Not checked / not available
Recommended Action: Check page reviews/recommendations and keep profile active
Priority: Medium

Realestate.com.au / Domain / RateMyAgent
Relevant: Yes — industry-specific proof
Current Status: Not checked yet
Recommended Action: Add these to future reputation monitoring
Priority: High

Current Status: No public listing found

Priority: Low

The AI should choose the checklist based on the detected business category.

Rules:
- Do not show TripAdvisor as highly relevant for real estate agencies.
- Do not punish a business if a platform is not relevant to its industry.
- If a platform is not checked, write “Not checked yet” instead of making up data.
- Use careful wording: “recommended”, “priority”, “secondary signal”.
- The report should explain that different industries rely on different review platforms.

Add a short explanation above the table:

“Not every review platform matters equally. This checklist shows which platforms are most relevant for your industry and where your reputation should be monitored next.”

Also update the Trust Score explanation:
“The Trust Score is calculated from available relevant review platforms. Platforms that are not relevant to this business category are not heavily weighted.”
