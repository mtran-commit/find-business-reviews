10. Trust Score calculation
The Trust Score must be calculated from the average of available ratings.

Use:
availableRatings = Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Review Pay if verified and available.

averageRating = sum(availableRatings) / availableRatings.length

trustScore = Math.round((averageRating / 5) * 100)

Example:
Google 4.5
Yelp 4.1
TripAdvisor 4.5
Average = 4.36
Trust Score = 87/100

If Review Pay has verified data, include it.
If Review Pay has no data, exclude it.
Do not punish the score when Review Pay is unavailable.
Do not include Facebook if unavailable.
