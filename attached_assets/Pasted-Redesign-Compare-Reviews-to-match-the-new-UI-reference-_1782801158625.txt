Trust Score calculation:
Calculate the Trust Score from available rating sources.

Use this logic:
1. Collect available ratings from:
   - Google
   - Yelp
   - TripAdvisor
   - Review Pay only if verified and available
2. Average the available ratings.
3. Convert to score out of 100:
   trustScore = Math.round((averageRating / 5) * 100)

Example:
Google 4.5
Yelp 4.1
TripAdvisor 4.5
Average = 4.36
Trust Score = 87/100

If Review Pay is available and verified, include it in the average.
If Review Pay is not available, do not punish the business.
If Facebook is unavailable, do not include it in the score.
