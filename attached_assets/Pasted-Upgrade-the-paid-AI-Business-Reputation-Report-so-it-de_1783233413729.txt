- Expected impact

Priority 1: Increase recent review volume
Why: Competitors have more reviews and this may influence customer trust.
Action: Ask recent satisfied clients for Google reviews within 48 hours of settlement.
Impact: Improves visibility, freshness and trust.

Priority 2: Use customer language in marketing
Why: Customers repeatedly mention auction, selling process and supportive team.
Action: Add those exact themes to the website and social proof sections.
Impact: Makes marketing match what customers already value.

Priority 3: Monitor secondary platforms
Why: Lower or missing ratings on secondary platforms can create doubt.
Action: Check TripAdvisor/Yelp only if relevant, but focus on high-relevance real estate platforms.
Impact: Reduces trust gaps.
