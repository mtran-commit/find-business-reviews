REPORT STRUCTURE

The final report must include these sections:

1. Report Header
Title:
AI Business Reputation Report

Subtitle:
Prepared for [Business Name]

Show:
- Date generated
- Data quality: High / Medium / Low
- Platforms checked: Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor
- Paid Report — $10 badge

2. KPI Cards
Show 4 cards:
- Trust Score
- Average Rating
- Total Reviews Analysed
- Customer Sentiment

Example:
Trust Score: 84/100
Average Rating: 4.3/5
Total Reviews Analysed: 428
Customer Sentiment: Mostly Positive

3. Executive Summary
Write a clear business summary:
- overall reputation
- main strengths
- main weaknesses
- what the business should focus on
- whether the business is competitive in the local area

4. Platform-by-Platform Comparison Table
Columns:
- Platform
- Average Rating
- Reviews Analysed
- Summary
- Business Meaning

Rows:
Google
Yelp
TripAdvisor

Example business meaning:
“Your strongest channel. Maintain momentum and keep generating fresh reviews.”

5. AI Customer Sentiment Analysis
Include:
- overall sentiment breakdown:
  positive %
  neutral %
  negative %
- positive themes
- negative themes
- AI insight box

If the AI cannot calculate percentages exactly, estimate carefully and label them as:
“Estimated from available review samples.”

6. Top Strengths Customers Mention
Show 3 cards:
- Strength theme
- Explanation
- Short example/evidence from review themes

Example:
Friendly and approachable staff
Customers consistently highlight the team as helpful, welcoming and professional.

7. Main Complaints and Risk Level
Show 3 cards:
- Complaint theme
- Risk level: Low / Medium / High
- Explanation
- Recommended fix

Example:
Slow response time
Risk level: Medium
Some customers mention delayed replies or follow-up.
Recommended fix: Set response SLA and monitor response times.

8. What May Be Costing You Customers
Show a list of potential conversion/reputation risks.

Examples:
- delayed first response after enquiry
- low review volume on secondary platforms
- competitors with stronger review consistency
- not enough recent reviews
- unclear offers or value proposition

9. Customer Language Insights
Show:
Words customers use:
- professional
- helpful
- knowledgeable
- responsive

Phrases to use in marketing:
- trusted local experts
- clear communication
- friendly service

Phrases/issues to avoid:
- slow response
- waiting for updates
- unclear timing
- poor communication

10. Competitor Snapshot
Show comparison table with 3 nearby competitors.

Columns:
- Competitor
- Trust Score
- Average Rating
- Reviews Analysed
- Comparison

Include a conclusion:
“You are competitive, but not the top-ranked business in your area.”
or
“You are currently ahead of most nearby competitors.”

11. Recommended Offer to Win More Bookings
Suggest one practical offer.

Examples:
For real estate:
Free local property appraisal + 15-minute strategy consultation

For restaurants:
Weekday lunch special or first-time visitor offer

For trades:
Free quote + priority callback

For beauty:
First visit treatment package or consultation offer

Include:
- Recommended offer
- Why it works
- Example copy

12. Review Improvement Opportunity
Show:
- priority: High / Medium / Low
- why it matters
- recommended action

Example:
Increasing recent reviews will significantly boost your trust score and visibility across platforms.

13. 7-Day Reputation Action Plan
Create 7 daily actions:
Day 1: Audit negative review themes
Day 2: Create faster follow-up template
Day 3: Ask recent happy customers for reviews
Day 4: Update website copy with customer language
Day 5: Train team on response standards
Day 6: Monitor competitor review gaps
Day 7: Launch recommended offer

14. 30-Day Reputation Plan
Create 4 weeks:
Week 1: Fix response process
Week 2: Collect more recent reviews
Week 3: Improve communication touchpoints
Week 4: Re-run report and adjust strategy

15. Suggested Response Templates
Include:
- positive review response template
- negative review response template

Make them professional and business-friendly.

16. Final Recommendation
Clear final recommendation:
- what the business should do first
- what could improve trust fastest
- what to monitor next

17. Disclaimer
Add:
“This report is generated using available public review data and AI analysis. It is intended for business insight purposes only and should not be treated as legal, financial or professional advice. Public review data may be incomplete or change over time.”
