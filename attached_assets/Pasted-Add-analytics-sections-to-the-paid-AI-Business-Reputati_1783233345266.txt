Add analytics sections to the paid AI Business Reputation Report.

The report should feel like a premium business intelligence report, not just a written review summary.

Add a new section called:

Reputation Analytics

Place it after the KPI cards and before the Executive Summary.

This section should include visual analytics cards:

1. Trust Score Trend
Show:
- Improving
- Stable
- Declining
- Not enough historical data

If historical data is not available, show:
“Trend tracking will begin from this report.”

2. Review Volume Analytics
Show:
- Total reviews analysed
- Estimated new reviews needed to compete with top nearby competitors
- Review volume compared with competitors

Example:
“Your business has 280 public reviews analysed. The top nearby competitor has 1,011 reviews. Increasing recent review volume may improve trust and conversion.”

3. Platform Rating Gap
Show:
- Highest platform rating
- Lowest platform rating
- Rating gap

Example:
“Google: 4.9/5”
“TripAdvisor: 3.8/5”
“Rating gap: 1.1 points”

Add explanation:
“A larger rating gap may cause customers to hesitate when comparing platforms.”

4. Sentiment Breakdown
Show:
- Positive %
- Neutral %
- Negative %
- Confidence level

Use a small donut chart or visual bars if possible.

5. Complaint Frequency
Show the top repeated complaints as analytics chips or bars.

Example:
- Slow response time: Medium frequency
- Communication gaps: Medium frequency
- Pricing expectation mismatch: Low frequency

6. Competitor Gap
Show:
- Your Trust Score
- Highest competitor Trust Score
- Difference

Example:
“Your Trust Score: 87”
“Top competitor: 98”
“Gap: 11 points”

7. Growth Opportunity Score
Create a score:
Low / Medium / High opportunity

Example:
“Growth Opportunity: High”
Reason:
“Strong ratings but lower review volume means the business may improve quickly by increasing recent reviews.”

Design:
Use dashboard-style cards:
- white background
- rounded corners
- subtle border
- purple icons
- dark navy text
- small progress bars
- clean metric labels

Use colours:
- Dark navy: #071A3D
- Purple: #7B3CFF
- Light purple: #F1E8FF
- Green: #16A34A
- Orange: #F97316
- Red: #DC2626
- Border: #E5E5E5

Do not use turquoise or aqua.
