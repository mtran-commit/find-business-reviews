QUALITY RULES

If platform data is missing:
Show “Not available” or “Not checked yet”
Do not invent data.

If review text is limited:
Show:
Data quality: Medium or Low
“This analysis is based on available public ratings, review counts and review samples.”

Industry-specific platform logic:
For real estate:
- Google is highly relevant
- Facebook may be relevant
- Realestate.com.au / Domain / RateMyAgent are highly relevant if available
- TripAdvisor is low relevance
- Yelp is low relevance in Australia

For restaurants:
- Google, TripAdvisor and Yelp are relevant
- Facebook/Instagram may be useful
- food platforms may be relevant

For trades:
- Google, Facebook, Hipages/Oneflare are relevant
- TripAdvisor is not relevant
